Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Translay AI ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Translay AI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Text for Translation: Only the text you actively choose to translate
  • Language Preferences: Your selected source and target languages (stored locally on your device)
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers

How We Use Information

  • Translation Processing: Text is sent securely to our translation service to provide accurate translations
  • Language Memory: Language preferences are stored locally on your device for convenience
  • No Storage: We do not store, retain, or log your translations on our servers

Data Security

  • All text is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • Text is processed immediately and deleted from our servers
  • No translation history is maintained
  • Language preferences remain on your device only

Third-Party Services

Translay AI uses a secure translation API to process your text. The text is:

  • Sent only for translation purposes
  • Not stored or retained by the translation service
  • Processed and immediately discarded

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Your translations are private and remain between you and the translation service.

Children's Privacy

Translay AI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for all ages.

Your Rights

  • Control: You control what text to translate
  • Deletion: Language preferences can be reset by deleting and reinstalling the app
  • No Account: No account creation means no stored personal data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
