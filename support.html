How to Use Translay AI

Using the Share Extension:

  1. Select text in any app (Safari, Messages, Notes, etc.)
  2. Tap the "Share" button
  3. Look for "Translay AI" in the share sheet
  4. Tap "Translay AI"
  5. Select your target language
  6. View your translation instantly
  7. Tap "Copy" to copy the translation

Using the Main App:

  1. Open Translay AI
  2. Type or paste text in the input area
  3. Select source and target languages
  4. Translation appears automatically
  5. Tap "Copy" to copy the result

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't see "Translay AI" in the share sheet. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  • Make sure you have selected text before tapping Share
  • Scroll right in the share sheet to find more options
  • Tap "More" or "..." to see additional apps
  • Restart your device if the extension still doesn't appear
Q: Which languages are supported?
A: Translay AI supports 18 languages:
  • English, Spanish, French, German
  • Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean
  • Arabic, Hindi, Italian, Dutch
  • Portuguese, Russian, Polish, Turkish
  • Ukrainian, Vietnamese
Q: Is my data private and secure?
A: Yes! Your privacy is our priority:
  • Text is only sent for translation purposes
  • No translations are stored on our servers
  • All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS
  • No personal information is collected
Read our full Privacy Policy
Q: The translation seems inaccurate. What can I do?
A: For best results:
  • Ensure the source text is clear and well-written
  • Try selecting the correct source language instead of "Detect"
  • Break down very long texts into smaller segments
  • Check for typos in the original text
Q: The app says "Translation failed." What's wrong?
A: This usually indicates a network issue:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try again in a few moments
  • Make sure you're not in airplane mode
  • Restart the app if the problem persists
Q: Can I use Translay AI offline?
A: No, Translay AI requires an internet connection to access our AI translation service for accurate, real-time translations.

Troubleshooting

App Not Working Properly

  • Force close and restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection

Share Extension Issues

  • Make sure text is selected before sharing
  • Try restarting your device
  • Check if the extension appears in other apps
  • Reinstall the app if problems persist

System Requirements

  • iOS Version: iOS 15.0 or later
  • Internet: Required for translations
  • Storage: Minimal space required
  • Compatibility: iPhone and iPad
